Muhammad ﷺ

The Messenger of Peace

Here's is a timeline of Prophet Muhammad ﷺ's Life:

  • 570 C.E - Born in Makkah and his father, ‘Abdullah dies before he's born.
  • 576 C.E - At only six years old, his mother also passed away.
  • 595 C.E - Married Khadija bint Khuwailid when he was 25 years old.
  • 610 C.E - Received revelation from Angel Gabriel.
  • 613-619 C.E. - The Prophet Muhammad initially kept the revelations private, confiding only in a small circle of family members and close friends.
  • 613-619 C.E. - Over time, the Prophet Muhammad began to preach to his own tribe members, and then throughout the city of Makkah.
  • 613-619 C.E. - The Makkan tribes organized a social boycott of the Muslims, not allowing people to trade with, care for, or socialize with the Muslims.
  • 619 C.E - The Prophet Muhammad’s beloved wife Khadija and his uncle/caretaker Abu Talib both died. Without Abu Talib’s protection, the Muslim community experienced increasing harassment in Makkah.
  • 622 C.E - The Prophet and his follower immigrated to Madina.
  • 624 C.E - Battle of Badr: Two years after the migration, the Makkan armies gathered outside of Madinah. The Muslims were outnumbered 3:1 but were successful in defending against the invading army.
  • 625 C.E - Battle of Uhud: the Makkans came back even stronger. This Battle was less decisive, and taught the Muslims an important lesson about over-confidence and greed.
  • 628 C.E - The Muslims conquered Makkah.
  • 632 C.E - The Prophet passes away.
